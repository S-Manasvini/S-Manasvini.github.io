Eclipsing Oblivion


“Poetry is an echo, asking a shadow to dance.” —Carl Sandburg

"These writings are for travellers lost in their memories,
in their labyrinth of highs and lows.
The pages serve as oasis,
Few comfort the soul while a few deceive"
Is death colorful?
S Manasvini

I assume death is colourful .Maybe like autumn,the crimson red hue that carpets the most dullest roads. Lingering and impending. At times I hope it looks like winter. Violet and blue skies showering chaste ivory on my windowpane. I wonder if the deep oceanic tone,make blueberries jealous,do they weep to doom or find solace that they shall finally rest under the same blue ?
I wish its both sombre and colourful. Deep tinted to make people engrossed with it, dull enough to not compete with life. It should change, from scarlet to ombre, a gentle gradient. Turbulence has tired me enough. I think about it to be a field of crocodile and fern,much alike the garden of Eden, where it all started.Sombre in hurricanes and sultry afternoons, whimsical with white daffodils aiming for the clouds and amber succulents reminding of times I stood out and rebelled.
It should have lilac undertones. Lilac like innocence ,piercing through my eyes. Wine and plum radiating out of nowhere reminding me of dreams that kept my nightmares away. Speckles of honey spots stab me like hope which keeps me awake while I am floating in vivid shades of life yearning to sink in a yard of graves.
"To die, to sleep
To sleep: perchance to dream: ay, there's the rub
For in that sleep of death what dreams may come
When we have shuffled off this mortal coil,
Must give us pause: there's the respect
That makes calamity of so long life."
— From "Hamlet"

Lost in the Tempest
S Manasvini

Through this windowpane
I glance at you,dear oak
for isn’t it cold out there.To be
amongst the unforgiving and fierce
and yet to hold on to your leaves
in a storm like this.
The storm whose eye and mine
are alike in many ways.Calm yet
unsettling while havoc rages
elsewhere, I glance numbingly
at you rooted below with the dirt
I shall unite with one dusk.
Till then I cradle my memories
through nooks and corners .For,alas!
one day they shall too fly away
in the winds of time leaving voids
my nightmares will attempt to fill.
Regardless I collect everything
predestined to drift away with the
passage of time patching old bruises
making space for wounds anew.
